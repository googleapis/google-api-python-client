Method Details

list(x__xgafv=None) 
Lists sites with Ad Experience Report statuses of "Reviewed - out of
compliance," "Reviewed - significantly out of compliance," or "Failed
review - egregious issues found."

Args:
  x__xgafv: string, V1 error format.
    Allowed values
      1 - v1 error format
      2 - v2 error format
